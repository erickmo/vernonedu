# Pages: Audience Segments

> "Untuk Siapa" pages — tailored messaging per audience.

---

## UX Strategy

Each audience sees VernonEdu through **their lens**:
- Different pain points, different benefits, different CTAs
- Same product, different framing
- Reduces cognitive load — visitor immediately sees "this is for me"

---

## Common Layout (all segment pages)

1. **Hero** — audience-specific headline + image + CTA
2. **Pain Points** — "Tantangan yang Anda hadapi" (3 cards)
3. **Solutions** — "Bagaimana VernonEdu membantu" (3-4 cards mapped to pain points)
4. **Relevant Programs** — course types most relevant to this audience
5. **Case Study / Testimonial** — from this audience type
6. **Partnership CTA** — collaboration form or contact

---

## Untuk Universitas

**Route:** `/untuk/universitas`
**Headline:** "Kolaborasi Kurikulum & Sertifikasi untuk Kampus Anda"

**Pain points:** Kurikulum tidak up-to-date, lulusan kurang siap kerja, butuh sertifikasi industri
**Solutions:** Joint program, guest lecturing, certification pathway, internship placement
**Relevant programs:** Kolaborasi dengan Universitas, Sertifikasi, Program Karir
**CTA:** "Ajukan Kerja Sama" → contact form with university-specific fields

---

## Untuk Sekolah

**Route:** `/untuk/sekolah`
**Headline:** "Program Pendamping Belajar untuk Siswa Sekolah"

**Pain points:** Ekstrakurikuler terbatas, siswa butuh skill tambahan, persiapan kuliah/kerja
**Solutions:** After-school programs, coding bootcamp, entrepreneurship intro, career guidance
**Relevant programs:** Kolaborasi dengan Sekolah, Kursus Reguler (youth-oriented)
**CTA:** "Hubungi Tim Kami" → contact form with school-specific fields

---

## Untuk Korporat

**Route:** `/untuk/korporat`
**Headline:** "Inhouse Training & Talent Development untuk Perusahaan"

**Pain points:** Employee skill gap, high training cost, hard to measure ROI
**Solutions:** Custom inhouse training, bulk pricing, progress tracking, certification
**Relevant programs:** Inhouse Training, Sertifikasi
**Pricing highlight:** Batch lump sum, negotiable pricing
**CTA:** "Minta Proposal" → contact form with company fields + estimated headcount

---

## Untuk Individu

**Route:** `/untuk/individu`
**Headline:** "Tingkatkan Skill, Raih Karir Impianmu"

**Pain points:** Tidak tahu harus mulai dari mana, butuh fleksibilitas, biaya terbatas
**Solutions:** Wide catalog, flexible schedule, multiple payment options, career support
**Relevant programs:** All (Program Karir, Reguler, Privat, Sertifikasi)
**CTA:** "Jelajahi Kursus" → `/katalog`
